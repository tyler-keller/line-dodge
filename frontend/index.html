HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Line Dodge!

Your goal is to survive and improve your score by dodging incoming line obstacles. With each round, the game gets progressively harder. Can you master all five rounds and set a new high score?

Game Rules:

  • Survive: Dodge lines to keep your lives and earn points.
  • Rounds: 5 rounds with increasing difficulty.
  • Power-Ups: Choose after each round:
    • Extra Life: +1 life.
    • Increased Stamina: Sprint longer.
    • Speed Boost: Move faster.
